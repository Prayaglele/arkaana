Our Approach Is Our Edge

We’ve designed the stack from the inside out with a holistic approach. We’re not just building features — we’re reimagining the hiring foundation.

Aria

“Aria” is short for Autonomous Recruitment Intelligence Agent, powered by agentic AI, to cut through the hiring red‑tape—making the execution around hiring intuitive, easy and fast by helping both the hiring manager and hiring team to generate competency‑based interview plans. Aria also steps in to fill in for mundane tasks like scheduling and calling, allowing recruiters to focus on the softer, human elements of hiring.

Interwoof

Our premium add‑on to Aria enhances the interview experience by being your Interview Companion to help provide real‑time prompts and post‑interview summaries that reduce bias, improve coverage, and ensure that each interview is meaningful. The time spent on hiring a resource should be a strategic investment, not a burden.

BlockCV

Verify and build trust in data. Evidence‑backed profiles, claim verification, and secure sharing to protect candidates and empower teams.

Frequently asked questions

How is Arkaana different from other AI hiring tools? ▾

We take a system view across the hiring lifecycle—role modeling, interviews, and verification—combining human judgment with AI, with what we call “positive integration”. It’s not a point solution; it’s a new foundation.

How do you reduce bias? ▾

Modular workflows customizable based on each company's internal workflows. Structured interview flows, competency‑based prompts, and evidence‑linked summaries reduce subjectivity. Human reviewers remain in control at each step.

How do you improve clarity and save time? ▾

By setting up Job Personas we allow each team to tailor job requirements for their roles, helping them build the clarity to find the right candidate. With agentic AI dealing with repetitive tasks we reduce human dependency—allowing AI to do what it's good at and humans to do what they're good at.
