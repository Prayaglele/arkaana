Frequently asked questions

How is Arkaana different from other AI hiring tools? ▾

We take a system view across the hiring lifecycle—role modeling, interviews, and verification—combining human judgment with auditable AI. It’s not a point solution; it’s a new foundation.

How do you reduce bias? ▾

Structured scorecards, competency‑based prompts, and evidence‑linked summaries reduce subjectivity. Human reviewers remain in control at each step.

What about privacy and security? ▾

Private by default. Data residency controls, role‑based access, and full audit trails. Candidate‑shared artifacts respect consent and revocation.
